# Data sources

## Online Job Advertisements (OJAs)
The corpus comes from the **Web Intelligence Hub (WIH)** initiative developed by **Eurostat** and **Cedefop** under the Trusted Smart Statistics framework.  [oai_citation:9‡GitHub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Crisp-Unimib/veuctor/main/README.md)

## ESCO taxonomy
Evaluation is grounded in **ESCO — European Skills, Competences, Qualifications and Occupations** (https://esco.ec.europa.eu/).  [oai_citation:10‡GitHub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Crisp-Unimib/veuctor/main/README.md)
