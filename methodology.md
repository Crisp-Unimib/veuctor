# VEUCTOR

**VEUCTOR** is a reproducible methodological framework for training, selecting, and aligning word embedding models built from European Online Job Advertisements (OJAs).  [oai_citation:3‡GitHub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Crisp-Unimib/veuctor/main/README.md)

## Paper
Colombo, E., D'Amico, S., Mercorio, F., Mezzanzanica, M. (2026).  
*VEUCTOR: Training and Selecting Best Vector Space Models from Online Job Ads for European Countries.* Preprint submitted to Elsevier.  [oai_citation:4‡GitHub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Crisp-Unimib/veuctor/main/README.md)

## Why it matters
Embedding choice is a methodological decision with measurable consequences for: occupational similarity structures, skill bundle composition, classification performance, wage/education prediction, and cross-country semantic comparability.
